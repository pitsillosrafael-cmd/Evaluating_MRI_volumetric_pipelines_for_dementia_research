# Evaluating_MRI_volumetric_pipelines_for_dementia_research
MSc thesis at the Cyprus Institute of Neurology and Genetics (2025). Evaluated most accessible and widely used volumetric pipelines for dementia research and clinical practise in terms of reproducibility and accuracy in detecting atrophy patterns in 3 dementia datasets (Allen dataset -robustness and agreement between the pipelines in a high quality research dataset, MIRIAD dataset -containing both AD and HC subjects for direct comparison between the groups, detection of the atrophy patterns and longitudinal study, The Cyprus Institute of Neurology and Genetics dataset -clinical dataset containing lower quality MRIs for plausibility of the tools to quantify the volumes in non-research MRIs)
